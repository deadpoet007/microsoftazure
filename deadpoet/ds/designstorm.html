INTRODUCTION

Design Storm , a designer cloth production company is being launched at e-commerce platform to abet budding designers to put forward their ideas and innovation , operating in Chandigarh. Our aim is to sell a range of clothes for children ,adults and mature . Design Storm assures the quality of clothes along with uniqueness of its style thereby enhancing their personality. We will target our market in Chandigarh initially and will gradually scale up our premises in Northern India so that these designers working at grassroot level get the appreciation of their work in the national market in form of cash and prosperity. We will be publicizing our brand through referrals , advertisements, pamphlets and community networking and research engine optimization. Design Storm will hold a promise of instant gratification for convenience and satisfaction of the customers. Initial investment required to launch the venture will be decided in the project meetings along with its future scope.


OBJECTIVES

To validate the minimum viable product (MVP) and take pragmatic step towards market actualization.
To learn Web development and Android app development.
To access market data and demand in the real world.
To understand the legal aspects to start an online venture.
To understand the rules and regulations required to form a company under the Companies Act, 1956.
To help budding designers to show their skills.
To analyze the different modes of online and offline publicity for the venture.
